About

A native of Northeast Ohio, Matthew was born, raised, and attended highschool in the Cleveland area.
He then went on to earn a B.S. in Sound Recording Technology from the DePaul University School of Music in Chicago, IL.

He currently resides in the Cleveland area, studying through the Free Code Camp program in hopes of developing marketable Front End and Back End development skills, while still maintaining his roots to music and audio recording.

To get in touch with Matthew, feel free to visit the Contact portion of this page, and get in touch through any of his various outlets.
